Omlet Recipes

Description

first take eggs and then break them in glass

now add salt and stir well

heat frying pan and put some oil now put the omlet on the frying pan and wait for 1 min YEEEAAAAH Omlet ready

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Break egg and put in glass
  2. add salt and onion and stir it well
  3. Heat the frying pan on medium flame and put oil
  4. Put the mixture in pan and wait for 1 min
  5. ENJOY !!!
